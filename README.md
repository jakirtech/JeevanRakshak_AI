# JeevanRakshak_AI 
Flood Health Intelligence System

An AI-powered disaster and public health platform for predicting, monitoring, and preventing disease outbreaks after floods — built for Assam and beyond.

🌍 Overview
JeevanRakshak AI (जीवनरक्षक — "Lifeguard" in Hindi/Assamese) is an advanced disaster and public health intelligence platform that combines AI prediction, real-time weather data, community crowdsourcing, and district-level risk analysis to deliver early warnings, safety guidance, and emergency support to flood-affected communities.

🎯 Transforming disaster management from reactive response to proactive prevention.

Built with a focus on Assam, one of India's most flood-prone states, the platform is designed to be accessible to all — including users in low-connectivity rural areas.

🎯 Core Objectives

🔴 Predict flood risk and disease outbreaks before they escalate

🚀 Key Features (Detailed)
🗺️ AI-Powered Flood Risk Map

District-wise visualization focused on Assam's 35 districts
Color-coded risk zones:

🟢 Safe — No immediate risk detected
🟡 Medium Risk — Monitor conditions closely
🔴 High Risk — Immediate action required


AI prediction powered by:

Rainfall intensity and forecast data
Water quality indicators
Historical flood pattern analysis




🧠 Intelligent Disease Prediction

Detects patterns from aggregated community symptom reports:

🤒 Fever · 💧 Diarrhea · 🤢 Vomiting · 😴 Fatigue


Predicts potential disease outbreaks (e.g., Cholera, Dengue, Typhoid)
Outputs a risk level with a confidence score


📱 Symptom Reporting System

Clean, mobile-first UI for easy health condition reporting
Anonymous data collection for community-level outbreak detection
Feeds directly into the AI prediction engine


📸 Community Reporting (Crowdsourced Intelligence)

Users can upload images of:

Flood conditions and waterlogging
Infrastructure damage (roads, bridges, buildings)


Enhances situational awareness for responders and authorities


🛡️ "Your Safety" Advisory Module

Disaster safety guidance: Flood, earthquake, animal protection
Health guidance: Post-flood diseases, first-aid tips
⚠️ Always advises users to consult a registered doctor before taking any medication.


🌦️ Advanced Weather System

Rainfall prediction and real-time tracking
Humidity and temperature monitoring
⚡ Lightning risk alerts
Weather-to-disease outbreak correlation engine


🎤 Voice Search (Multi-language)

Fully hands-free search experience
Supported languages:

🇬🇧 English
🇮🇳 Hindi
🏳️ Assamese (অসমীয়া)


Improves accessibility for non-literate and elderly users


🚨 Emergency Support System

One-tap emergency contact buttons:

🚑 Ambulance
👮 Police
🆘 Disaster Relief Helpline
🐄 Animal Rescue


Location-based assistance routing


📊 Admin Dashboard

Real-time monitoring of health and flood data across all districts
Graphs, trends, and analytics for decision-making
Zone-level alert broadcasting
Data export for government reports and research


🤖 AI Chat Assistant

Answers natural-language queries such as:

"What should I do during a flood?"
"What are the symptoms of Cholera?"
"Nearest hospital in Dibrugarh?"




📡 Offline Mode

Fully functional in low or no-connectivity areas
Stores reports and data locally on the device
Automatically syncs to the server when connectivity is restored


🛡️ Disclaimer

⚠️ JeevanRakshak AI is a decision-support tool and does not replace professional medical advice. All health-related guidance in the app is informational only. Users are always advised to consult a qualified doctor or medical professional before taking any medication or making health decisions.
